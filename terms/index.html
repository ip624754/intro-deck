1. Service scope

Intro Deck provides a directory of active, listed member profiles, a profile workflow, a single contact-request entry point with free intro, private-chat, and Telegram-contact options, plus approved private chat inside Telegram. Listed profile cards may be browsed by bot users; private contact details are not public by default.

2. LinkedIn connection and profile claims

LinkedIn sign-in connects an account identity and may provide basic identity data such as name, photo, locale, or email when available. Verified on LinkedIn may separately return category-level signals that LinkedIn has confirmed identity or a workplace association. Those signals do not verify a member-entered title, role, seniority, skills, experience, expertise, or the accuracy of the rest of the card. Members remain responsible for the profile information they publish.

3. Verification use limitations

Verified on LinkedIn is used only as a trust-enhancement signal. It is not a background check, government identity check, regulated KYC service, employment screening tool, credit or insurance decision tool, or a substitute for Intro Deck's own safety and abuse controls. Intro Deck must not use the signal as the sole basis for hiring, eligibility, risk scoring, or other high-impact decisions.

4. Acceptable use

  • Do not misuse the service for spam, scraping, fraud, harassment, or unlawful activity.
  • Do not attempt to bypass directory, contact, entitlement, or access controls.
  • Do not interfere with the security or reliability of the service.

5. User content and profile data

You are responsible for the profile information and request content you submit. You should only provide information that you are allowed to share and that is accurate to the best of your knowledge.

6. Contact requests and Telegram Stars

A paid private-chat or Telegram-contact option purchases delivery of a permission request to another member. It does not purchase the member's contact details, guarantee approval, guarantee a reply, or create a right to continue contacting that member. The recipient may approve, decline, block, or report the request.

Unless Intro Deck explicitly states otherwise before payment, a request-delivery fee is not automatically refunded only because the recipient declines, does not reply, or the request remains unanswered. This does not limit any refund or remedy that may be required by applicable law or provided by Telegram's payment rules.

7. Pro fair use

An active Pro subscription may include a bounded rolling allowance for private-chat and Telegram-contact request deliveries. Pro does not provide unlimited outreach, does not bypass recipient approval, and may be limited or suspended when activity indicates spam, harassment, automation, evasion of controls, or other abuse. The current allowance is shown in the bot before purchase and on the Pro status screen.

8. Availability and changes

Intro Deck may add, change, limit, or remove features at any time. Access may be suspended or limited to protect users, enforce policy, or maintain service integrity. Material changes to paid request terms or Pro allowances should be shown in the product before a new purchase.

9. Contact

For product or legal questions, contact the operator through the official Intro Deck LinkedIn page or the official Telegram bot at @introdeckbot.
