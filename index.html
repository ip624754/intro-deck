FAQ

What people usually want to know.

What is Intro Deck?

Intro Deck is a Telegram-native professional directory. Listed member cards can be browsed in the bot; contact details and private continuation remain approval-based.

Who can see listed profiles?

Active, listed profile cards are visible to bot users. Telegram handles and other private contact details are not public by default.

Why use LinkedIn?

LinkedIn connects an account identity and may separately return identity or workplace verification categories. Those categories do not verify member-entered roles, companies, skills, experience, or expertise.

How does contact work?

An intro request is sent directly to the profile owner; it is not a third-party introduction. Paid actions purchase request delivery, not approval or a reply.

What happens after I open the bot?

You connect a LinkedIn account, complete a member-provided card, browse listed profiles, and request the contact path allowed by the profile owner.

Can I share my profile on LinkedIn?

Yes. For an active, listed profile, Intro Deck shows the exact post first. Nothing is published until you explicitly authorize that one share on LinkedIn, and Intro Deck does not store the OAuth access token.
