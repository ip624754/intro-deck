# STEP064B4D1A — Compact Above-the-Fold Profile Share

## Product objective

Make the ordinary LinkedIn profile-share useful before LinkedIn collapses the body behind `…see more`.

## Canonical structure

1. Short Intro Deck permission-positioning paragraph.
2. Bounded focus/fallback sentence plus public profile CTA.

## Content rules

- Do not repeat member identity already visible in LinkedIn's author header.
- Do not repeat full headline, company, or About.
- Render at most three skill labels.
- Use localized industry/fallback text when skills are absent.
- Do not use decorative emoji.
- Keep the neutral CTA arrow `→`.
- Preserve the exact Telegram preview → published commentary contract.

## Non-goals

- Guaranteed suppression of LinkedIn `…see more`.
- Media/image upload.
- Dynamic AI rewriting.
- Changes to OAuth, publisher, callbacks, payment, AI/news, schema, or ENV.
